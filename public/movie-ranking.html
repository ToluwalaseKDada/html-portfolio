Lase's Top 3 Rated Movies

My Top 3 Movies of All Time.

Dangal

Very solid, very sound entertainment, with thumpingly good Pritam songs that make Eye of the Tiger seem like pipsqueakery. Haha. This review was by The Guardian and I can agree with it

Three Idiots

This movie conveys a valuable message about friendship and had interesting, funny scenes that just makes it totally enjoyable.

My Name is Khan

An Indian Muslim man with Asperger's syndrome takes a challenge to speak to the President of the United States seriously and embarks on a cross-country journey. It was a sad story in soeme parts but had a beautiful end to it
